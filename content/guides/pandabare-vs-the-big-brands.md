# PandaBare vs the big brands: an honest look at where we fit

PandaBare is a small Australian brand focused on bamboo socks, wristbands, sleep masks and travel comfort, not a full loungewear range. Compared with a larger player like Boody, we make less, but we curate it tightly, especially through the Comfort Kit for sleep, travel and gifting. If you want a full bamboo wardrobe, a bigger brand may suit you better.

That's the short version. Here's the longer, more honest one.

## Who are the big bamboo brands in Australia?

Australia has a handful of established bamboo apparel brands. [Boody](https://www.boody.com.au/pages/about-us) was founded in Sydney in 2012 by David Greenblo and Neil Midalia, and has grown into a full range of bamboo basics sold across 15 countries, including underwear, socks, activewear and loungewear. [Bamboozld](https://www.bamboozld.com.au/) launched in Melbourne in 2014 with a similar basics focus, but leans into playful, patterned designs and sells through retailers like David Jones and The Iconic. [Mongrel Socks](https://www.mongrelsocks.com/collections/bamboo-socks) takes a different angle again, built by NSW workboot maker Victor Footwear, with bamboo work socks aimed squarely at tradies and long shifts on your feet.

PandaBare sits alongside these, not against them. We're newer, smaller, and we've chosen to stay narrow: socks, wristbands, sleep and travel essentials, built around one hero bundle rather than a full wardrobe.

## Is PandaBare vs Boody even a fair comparison?

Not entirely, and that's worth saying plainly. Boody has been trading since 2012, holds [B Corp certification](https://goodonyou.eco/boody-bamboo-basics/), and sells a genuinely wide range: tees, dresses, leggings, activewear, underwear and socks for men, women and babies. That's a different scale of business to PandaBare.

Where the comparison is fair is on the thing we both make: bamboo socks. There, you can compare fabric, price and fit directly. Where it's not fair is expecting PandaBare to match Boody's breadth. We don't try to. Our bet is that a tighter range, built around comfort and gifting occasions, is more useful for a specific kind of shopper than trying to be a smaller version of everything Boody does.

## Is Boody good quality, and should that change your decision?

Reviews suggest yes, mostly. Boody holds a [4.4 out of 5 rating from over 700 reviews on ProductReview.com.au](https://www.productreview.com.au/listings/boody) and a 4-star average on [Trustpilot](https://au.trustpilot.com/review/www.boody.com.au) from close to 700 reviews. Customers consistently praise the softness of the fabric and the responsiveness of customer service.

Some more recent reviews mention thinner fabric on certain lines and occasional pilling, alongside a few notes on sizing consistency. That's a normal spread for a brand operating at Boody's scale and doesn't mean the products are poor. It does mean it's worth reading a few recent reviews for the specific item you're after, rather than assuming every product performs identically.

None of this is a knock on Boody. It's a genuinely well-regarded brand, and if you want bamboo underwear, activewear or dresses, it's a sensible place to look, because PandaBare doesn't make those things.

## What does PandaBare actually make, and what does that mean for you?

Our range is short on purpose: bamboo crew socks, bamboo ankle socks, sport wristbands, a sleep mask, a travel pouch, and the [PandaBare Comfort Kit](/comfort-kit), which bundles a sleep mask, travel pouch and socks into one set built for sleep, travel or giving. We also do gift packs and corporate gifting for businesses that want something better than a branded pen.

That's the whole shop. No leggings, no underwear, no dresses. If you already have a drawer of bamboo tees and just need something for a flight, a hotel stay, or a gift you don't want to overthink, that narrowness is the point. If you're after a full wardrobe switch to bamboo, a broader brand like Boody will do more of the job in one place.

## Where does PandaBare genuinely fit compared to Bamboozld and Mongrel?

Against Bamboozld, the difference is mostly about breadth and design language. Bamboozld leans into fun prints and a wider retail footprint through stores like [David Jones](https://www.davidjones.com/brand/bamboozld) and The Iconic. PandaBare stays plainer and more muted, and puts more weight on the sleep and travel occasion than on sock design as a personality statement.

Against Mongrel Socks, the comparison barely applies. Mongrel is built for tradies and long days in boots, sold through workwear retailers under a company that's [made work boots in Australia since 1930](https://www.mongrelsocks.com/). PandaBare is built for slower moments: bed, a plane seat, a gift box. Different job, different sock.

## What's the honest reason to pick PandaBare?

If you want one curated set for sleep and travel rather than assembling it piece by piece, the [Comfort Kit](/comfort-kit) is the clearest reason. It's built as one thing, not a collection you have to build yourself from a large catalogue. The same logic applies to our [gift packs](/gift-packs) and [corporate gifting](/corporate-gifting): fewer decisions, still genuinely useful.

We're not going to tell you PandaBare beats Boody on range, certifications or years in business, because it doesn't. What we'd say instead: if your need is specific (a gift, a flight, a better night's sleep) a small curated range can be a faster, less overwhelming choice than a large one. That's the gap we're built to fill. ## FAQ

**Is PandaBare the same as Boody?**
No. Boody is a larger, Sydney-founded brand with a full range of bamboo basics sold in 15 countries. PandaBare is a smaller, newer brand focused only on socks, wristbands, sleep and travel comfort, built around the Comfort Kit.

**Is Boody good quality?**
Most reviews rate it highly for softness and comfort, with a 4.4 star average from over 700 reviews on ProductReview.com.au. Some recent reviews mention thinner fabric or pilling on certain items, so it's worth reading current reviews for the specific product you want.

**What is the difference between Boody and Bamboozld?**
Boody is older and larger, founded in 2012 with a full basics range sold internationally. Bamboozld launched in 2014 in Melbourne and focuses more tightly on socks and underwear with a playful design style, sold through department stores and workwear retailers.

**Why choose PandaBare over a bigger bamboo brand?**
Choose PandaBare if you want a small, curated set of comfort essentials for travel, sleep or gifting, rather than a full wardrobe of bamboo basics. The Comfort Kit bundles socks, sleep mask and travel pouch as one gift-ready package.

**Does PandaBare make loungewear or underwear like Boody does?**
Not currently. Our range stays to socks, wristbands, sleep mask, travel pouch, the Comfort Kit, and gifting. For bamboo underwear, tees or activewear, a full-range brand like Boody is the better fit.
